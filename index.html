Syarat & Ketentuan PerdaganganTrade Terms & Conditions

1. Sifat Penawaran (Price Validity)1. Nature of Quotation (Price Validity)
Mengingat volatilitas pasar komoditas agrikultur dan biomassa global, semua dokumen *Quotation* (Penawaran Harga) yang diterbitkan PT WAYGO memiliki batas waktu validitas yang mengikat (umumnya 7-14 hari kerja). Setelah masa tersebut, harga dapat dievaluasi ulang berdasarkan indeks pasar terkini. Given the volatility of the global agriculture and biomass commodity markets, all Quotation documents issued by PT WAYGO have a binding validity period (typically 7-14 working days). After this period, prices may be re-evaluated based on current market indices.

2. Ketentuan Incoterms & Logistik2. Incoterms & Logistics Terms
Kami melayani pengiriman berbasis regulasi Incoterms 2020. Penyerahan barang umumnya dilakukan melalui basis *FOT (Free On Truck)* di pabrik klien untuk domestik, atau *FOB (Free On Board) / CIF* untuk kebutuhan ekspor. Risiko kerusakan berpindah sesuai terminologi Incoterms yang disepakati dalam kontrak (MoU/PO). We facilitate shipments based on Incoterms 2020 regulations. Handover of goods is generally done on an FOT (Free On Truck) basis at the client's factory for domestic, or FOB (Free On Board) / CIF for export needs. Risk of damage transfers according to the agreed Incoterm terminology in the contract (MoU/PO).

3. Prosedur Pembayaran (Terms of Payment)3. Terms of Payment
Untuk menjamin arus kas yang sehat dalam pengadaan bahan baku skala grosir, kami menerapkan sistem pembayaran Transfer Bank (T/T) ke rekening perusahaan resmi. Skema *Down Payment* (DP) diwajibkan sebelum proses *loading* (pemuatan) untuk mengunci ketersediaan barang. Opsi Letter of Credit (L/C) *at sight* yang tidak dapat dibatalkan (Irrevocable) dari bank prima (Prime Bank) dapat didiskusikan untuk transaksi ekspor skala masif. To guarantee healthy cash flow in wholesale raw material procurement, we implement a Bank Transfer (T/T) payment system to the official corporate account. A Down Payment (DP) scheme is strictly required prior to the loading process to lock in stock availability. Irrevocable Letter of Credit (L/C) at sight from a prime bank can be negotiated for massive-scale export transactions.

4. Klaim Susut / Quality Dispute4. Shrinkage / Quality Dispute Claims
Hasil final untuk tonase (berat) merujuk pada Tiket Timbangan Bersama (Weighbridge Ticket) di titik bongkar, dengan toleransi penyusutan alamiah yang disepakati (misal: penguapan material basah). Perselisihan mengenai spesifikasi kualitas (Quality Dispute) akan diselesaikan berdasarkan uji sampling dari laboratorium independen yang disetujui bersama. Final results for tonnage refer to the Joint Weighbridge Ticket at the discharge point, with an agreed natural shrinkage tolerance (e.g., evaporation of wet materials). Quality Disputes regarding specifications will be resolved based on sampling tests from a mutually approved independent laboratory.
